PROCESS

Every project is going to be different, but this is the usual structure that I like to follow as best practices.

RESEARCH
Understand the client’s goals and objectives of the project. Along with developing mood boards based off of the client’s vision.
CONCEPT
Use the research and develop 2-3 rough concepts. Then review with the client to finalize a direction.
DESIGN
The designing and production phase, where all the assets are developed or prototyped to be reviewed by the client.
REVIEW
Back and forth feedback with the client regarding design or copy changes before the finalization of the assets. Up to 2-3 versions of proofs.
FINALIZE
Once the project is approved, all the assets will be finalized, files packaged up and sent off to the client. Continuing support is always an option.
