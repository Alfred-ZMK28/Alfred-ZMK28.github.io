Robertson College

Project type

Mural

Role

Illustrator

Tools Used

Procreate & Adobe Illustrator

year

2023

Robertson is a post-secondary college with several campuses across Canada, that provide condensed programs for students on site or remote learning. Their main target audience is students who are new to Canada, so the college wanted a mural in their Winnipeg campus, to showcase their 'Robertson Global' programs.

This mural design was a collaborative effort with the studio Little Ghost Creative and myself. The concept consists of illustrations of diverse students, and iconography that represents studying and the country, and typography of encouraging words in multiple languages. All together embodying the program of Roberson Global. The style of the mural reflects the iconography used in the Robertson branding.
